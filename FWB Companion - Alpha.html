      • Flight Weather Brief Companion
      The FWB Companion attempts to consolidate the most frequently utilized WX resources into a single page. The forecaster must first paste the ICAO, Date, and Time formats provided by JET into the texts areas on the left and click sort. The data is then parsed into an input field in the lower portion of the page. The parameters can then be pulled from to retrieve mission specific WX products with just a few clicks.
